Personal Profile

PhD student in Finance at Sun Yat-sen University Lingnan College, visiting scholar at The University of Hong Kong. Main research areas include digital finance, financial risks, and green finance. Published over 20 papers in journals such as Financial Review, International Review of Financial Analysis, Technological Forecasting & Social Change, Business Strategy and the Environment (2 articles), Energy Economics (2 articles), Financial Innovation, etc., with several papers selected as ESI highly cited (5 articles) and ESI hot papers (7 articles). Cited over 900 times with an H-index of 16. Several working papers are under revision in journals like Research Policy, Journal of Corporate Finance. Lead researcher for the Sun Yat-sen University graduate academic innovation project "Financial Risk Early Warning System in the Digital Finance Era". Member of the AEA and AFA. Reviewer for over 30 SSCI journals including Applied Economics, Business Strategy and the Environment, Energy Economics, International Review of Financial Analysis, and Managing Editor for Journal of Economic Analysis and Economic Analysis Letters.
